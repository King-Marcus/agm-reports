6Amazon Program & Tactic Gaps

Areas where Triquetra is leveraging Amazon features that 10X Health isn't fully exploiting:

📦 Subscribe & Save

Triquetra's 5-MTHF Subscribe & Save price is $19.79/month vs $21.99 one-time. This drives recurring revenue and locks in customers long-term. S&S customers convert at 3-5x higher LTV. If 10X Health's 5-MTHF is not enrolled, this is immediate lost recurring revenue versus Triquetra's locked-in subscriber base.

Action: Enroll all 10X Health Amazon products in Subscribe & Save. Set auto-delivery discount at 10-15%.

🎬 Product Videos (A+ Content)

Triquetra has 11 product videos on their 5-MTHF listing. Video content increases conversion rates 3-4x and Amazon prioritizes listings with video content in search. Videos explain the methylation story, MTHFR gene, and product benefits in ways static images cannot.

Action: Add minimum 3 product videos to each 10X Health Amazon listing. Gary Brecka testimonial videos would be highly effective.

🏪 Brand Store Architecture

Triquetra's brand store has 23 health-concern navigation categories, a "Busy Mom Starter Kit" bundle, a "NEW" tab, and a "BEST SELLERS" tab. This dramatically improves discoverability and average order value. 10X Health's brand store likely lacks this structure.

Action: Rebuild 10X Health's Amazon brand store with concern-based navigation. Create "Gary's Daily Stack" or "Performance Bundle" featured collections.

🌍 International Marketplaces

Triquetra is active in US, Singapore, and UAE Amazon marketplaces (shown in H10 data). This diversifies revenue and captures international supplement buyers. The UAE market in particular has low competition in premium supplements.

Action: Evaluate international marketplace expansion — start with Canada (lowest barrier) then UAE for premium supplement positioning.

🎯 Sponsored Brand — SERP Ownership

Triquetra owns the Sponsored Brand banner for "5-mthf" with the headline "The Metabolically Active Form of Folate." This grabs the entire top-of-page position before any organic results. 10X Health should run SB ads for their key terms — especially "methylated multivitamin" and "5-mthf."

Action: Activate Sponsored Brand campaigns for "5-mthf", "methylated multivitamin", and "MTHFR supplement" with Gary Brecka lifestyle imagery.

🧒 Kids Category Presence

Triquetra's Kids Methylfolate + B12 generates $291K/month with 8,000+ units/month. This is a completely separate buyer demographic (parents) that 10X Health's current children's gummies don't fully serve — the gummies have only 71 reviews vs. 2,427 for Triquetra's kids liquid. A targeted parent-focused listing could capture this revenue.

Action: Invest in aggressive review generation for 10X Children's Gummies. Add pediatrician-recommended messaging. Consider Kids Liquid dropper variant.
